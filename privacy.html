Privacy Policy — Simple Trip Planner

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This privacy policy describes how Simple Trip Planner (“the App”) handles information.

Overview

Simple Trip Planner organizes trips on your device and, when you choose to sign in, syncs trip data to Google Firebase so you can use the same trips across devices and invite others to collaborate.

Signing in is optional. Without an account, trip data stays on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework.

Accounts

If you sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google, we store:

  • Your account identifiers provided by the sign-in provider (user ID, email when available, display name, profile photo URL)
  • Authentication session information managed by Firebase Authentication

You can sign out at any time from Settings → Account.

Trip data

When you are signed in, trip content you create or edit (destinations, dates, activities, packing lists, expenses, notes, and document/ticket files) is stored in Cloud Firestore and Firebase Storage. Invited members of a trip can view and edit the same trip data. Everyone invited has equal access.

When you are signed out, that content remains only on your device unless you export or share it yourself.

Network services

The App may contact:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, cloud trip storage, and file storage when you are signed in
  • Apple WeatherKit — weather forecasts for trip destinations you enter (destination names are geocoded; the App does not request your device GPS location for this feature)
  • jsDelivr CDN — a public country / flag list for the country picker
  • Apple / Google — during Sign in with Apple or Google Sign-In

The App does not send your trip documents or ticket PDFs to third-party AI services.

Notifications

With your permission, the App schedules local reminders on your device (for example packing or trip start). These are not delivered through our servers.

Share extension

If you use the Share Extension to send a PDF into the App, the file is passed to the App on your device via an App Group container. If you are signed in, attaching it to a trip may upload it to Firebase Storage as part of that trip.

Account deletion

To delete your account and associated cloud profile data, contact the developer via the support email on the App Store product page, or email [you@email.com]. Trip membership and shared trip content may remain available to other members of those trips.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date above will change when we do.
